Services

My practice is focused on women and their unique issues includeing:

  • Anxiety and depression, relationship issues and divorce, separation and divorce
  • High risk pregnancy, pregnancy loss, post partum depression, adjusting to motherhood, parenting children with special needs
  • Challenges and adjustments related to health issues
  • Grief/loss and adjustment as experienced in death, divorce and aging.

I understand that some health or life issues may make it difficult to come to my office. I am willing to make home visits for certain conditions such as pregnancy bed rest, new mother with infant, and recouping from medical treatment such as breast cancer etc.
